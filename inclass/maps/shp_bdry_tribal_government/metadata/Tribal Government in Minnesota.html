Section 1: Overview

Originator:Minnesota Department of Transportation

Title: Tribal Government in Minnesota

Abstract: Tribal Government boundaries is a layer that contains the Minnesota interpretation of the federally recognized tribal boundaries within Minnesota. For the Federal interpretation see the US senses TIGER files.

Purpose: This layer was created to represent the general boundaries of the 12 Tribal lands in the State of Minnesota for cartographic mapping and planning-level spatial analysis. This layer is derived from legal descriptions, but should not be used for survey-grade applications.

Time Period of Content Date: 2023-11-06

Currentness Reference: Data is complete through end of construction for date referenced. Construction subsequent to said date is in process to being entered and may or may not be included.
